FAQ

What will be my delivery cost?

The delivery fee for typical orders is Rs.350/-, which will be added to the entire order amount. The delivery cost for same-day delivery orders is Rs.550/-

How do I change or cancel an order?

Only 2 days before the delivery date may you amend or cancel an order that has been confirmed. If you get an item and decide you want to swap it, you can bring it to any of our Trendz locations. For assistance, please contact one of our customer service representatives, who will gladly assist you with your request. For further details, please see our Returns Policy.

Will I receive confirmation of my order?

Yes, you will be notified via e-mail and sms.

Your order did not arrive?

Every normal purchase will be delivered in 4-5 business days. If your order has not arrived, please contact us by phone or email as soon as possible. Tel: (+94)11 2345678 Email: trendz.lk@gmail.com

What if I have received incorrect or faulty good?

We regret that your delivery did not go as planned. To learn how to return an item to us, please see our Return Policy. Please contact us if your purchase arrives damaged.
